Basa Pano Job Market Place (Site Plan)

Site Plan

Site Name

Basa Pano Job Market Place shall be the name of the site. The name is a Shona translation of the phase "Work Here".

Site Purpose

The site is intended to help make online jobs avialable to those seeking employment and also bringing the applicants to the hiring employer. The site will offer a platform to market available posts for jobs of all kinds that can be done both remotely or on cite

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A company is actively seeking for web-developers at inturn level who have at least complete 2 years of post high school education and are working towards attaining a bachelor honours degree in any IT related field.

Website Content Response: The hiring company can enter all their requirements for one to get the job and their preferences. Many people will view the job posting and application instructions are provided.


Scenario 2: A Data Science student at a local University is actively seeking for a work related learning experience and has been waiting on job posting on all other platform but most of the postings require College graduates.

Website Content Response: The website indicates the level of qualificatons required and the student can filter which job descriptions best fit their needs

Color Schema

I will use a Midnight-Green colour for the header and footer, Black for the navigation and Tiffany-Blue for my headings background.

These colors are relavant to Kwekwe as it is surrounded by rivers and green vegetation. They are also friendly to the visually impaired users as they as not so shouting and blend in well with the browser default white or black font colors.
