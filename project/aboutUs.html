About Us

BaSa PaNo was founded in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic as a solution for job seekers. Recognizing the challenges brought on by the pandemic, BaSa PaNo aimed to connect people with job opportunities in a time when traditional job markets were disrupted. Our platform continues to help job seekers find relevant opportunities and career resources.
